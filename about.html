about our project

Welcome to MaktabetCom. For We are working to capture the hearts of readers of the readers. We believe that access to information is a fundamental right, and our mission is to empower, educate, and inspire every person who walks through our doors or visits us online.


What We Offer

  • Explore Our Collection: From timeless classics to the latest bestsellers, academic journals to graphic novels, our physical shelves are stocked to fuel your imagination.
  • The Digital Library (Read & Download): Your library card is your passport to a massive digital world. Access thousands of eBooks, magazines, and research papers from the comfort of your home. Download them to your tablet, phone, or e-reader instantly.
  • Events & Workshops: We are a hub for connection. Join us for weekly storytimes for kids, teen coding workshops, adult book clubs, and guest author lectures. Check our calendar to see what’s happening this week.
